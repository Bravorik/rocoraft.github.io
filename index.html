Privacy Policy

Your trust is our code — we protect it

At Rocoraft, your privacy matters as much as clean code. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it. Last updated: April 2025.

What We Collect

When you fill out the Join form, we collect:

  • Your name and email address (for communication)
  • Phone number and city (optional, for community events)
  • Your experience level (to personalize content for you)

How We Use It

  • To send you updates about Rocoraft content and launches
  • To understand our community and improve what we make
  • We will never sell your data to third parties

Cookies & Analytics

This site may use basic analytics (like Vercel Analytics) to understand traffic patterns. No invasive tracking. No ad targeting. No surveillance capitalism.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us through any of our social handles. We will remove your data within 7 days, no questions asked.

Third-Party Links

Links to YouTube, Instagram, and X are subject to their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review them independently.
