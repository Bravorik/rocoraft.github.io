Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 — Effective immediately

Rocoraft is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, how we use it and your rights as a visitor. We believe every reader deserves full transparency.

1. Who we are

Rocoraft is a free comic e-book platform founded by Soumodeep Patra in 2025. Our website is located at rocoraft.github.io and our contact email is rocoraft.business@gmail.com. We provide free access to comic e-books across multiple genres for readers worldwide.

2. What information we collect

We collect minimal information to improve your reading experience. This may include:

  • Basic analytics data — pages visited, comics read, time spent, device type and country via Google Analytics
  • Email address — only if you contact us directly via email
  • Username and password — only if you create an account on our platform, stored encrypted and secure
  • Reading preferences — genres and comics you interact with, to improve recommendations

We do not collect sensitive personal information like financial data, government IDs or medical information.

3. How we use your information

Any information collected is used only for the following purposes:

  • To understand how readers use our platform and improve our content library
  • To respond to your messages or partnership requests
  • To manage your account on our platform
  • To recommend comics based on your reading history — only with your consent

We will never sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing and reading experience. These include:

  • Analytics cookies — from Google Analytics to track page views and traffic
  • Session cookies — to keep you logged in if you have an account
  • Preference cookies — to remember your genre preferences and reading progress

You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that some features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

5. Third party services

Rocoraft uses the following third party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — for website traffic analysis
  • GitHub Pages — for website hosting

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services separately.

6. Your rights

As a visitor or user of Rocoraft, you have the following rights:

  • The right to know what personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data at any time
  • The right to opt out of analytics tracking
  • The right to update or correct your account information

To exercise any of these rights, email us at rocoraft.business@gmail.com and we will respond within 7 working days.

7. Children's privacy

Rocoraft offers content for all ages including younger readers. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our platform grows. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to check this page periodically.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out:

rocoraft.business@gmail.com

We are committed to responding to all privacy related queries within 7 working days.
