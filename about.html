Our Story

The story behind Rocoraft

A startup born from a love of comics and a belief that every great story deserves a great audience — for free.

RC

Soumodeep Patra

Founder and CEO — Rocoraft

Soumodeep Patra is a passionate storyteller, entrepreneur and digital creator who grew up reading comics and dreaming of a world where great stories are accessible to everyone. With years of experience in digital content and technology, Soumodeep founded Rocoraft in 2025 with a bold mission — to become India's leading free comic e-book platform and bring the magic of sequential storytelling to millions of readers.

The Journey

How Rocoraft began

It started with a simple observation — comic books were expensive, hard to find, and often locked behind paywalls. Millions of readers, especially students and young people in India, were missing out on incredible stories simply because they could not afford them.

Soumodeep decided to change that. Drawing on a lifelong love of comics and a background in digital technology, the idea for Rocoraft was born — a free, accessible, beautifully designed platform where anyone could discover and read comic e-books across every genre imaginable.

Rocoraft is not just a platform — it is a movement. A movement that believes stories have the power to inspire, heal, excite and connect people across every boundary.

Timeline

Our milestones

2022

The spark

Soumodeep begins collecting and digitising rare comic books, noticing the massive gap in free accessible content for Indian readers.

2024

The idea takes shape

Research begins into building a proper platform. The name Rocoraft is born — combining story, craft and the spirit of adventure.

2025

Rocoraft launches

Rocoraft officially goes live with its first catalogue of 100+ comic e-books across 6 genres — completely free for all readers.

2026

Growing fast

Rocoraft crosses 10,000 readers, expands its catalogue to 500+ titles and begins partnerships with independent comic artists and writers.
